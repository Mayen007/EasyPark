About Us

Innovating the Parking Experience

Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a passion to simplify urban parking, EASY PARK emerged from personal frustration with traditional parking systems.

Our journey began in Nairobi's bustling CBD, where our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of finding reliable parking. What started as a university project has now transformed into Kenya's leading parking solution platform.

Key Milestones
  • 2020: Launched MVP with 5 parking lots
  • 2021: Reached 10,000 active users
  • 2022: Expanded to 3 major cities
  • 2023: Won National Innovation Award
